## 1. The Strategic Vision of Prof. Dae-Sik Kim
Professor Dae-Sik Kim (KAIST) interprets AI not merely as a tool, but as a structural shift in the human experience. His insights can be distilled into three pillars:

* **From Answers to Questions:** In an era where LLMs provide instant answers, human value shifts to **"Critical Inquiry"** and the ability to define goals.
* **The 5-Year AGI Window:** AI's exponential growth and "Weight Matrix" knowledge transfer suggest human-level intelligence is imminent, requiring urgent preparation.
* **The "Roman Empire" Analogy:** As AI automates labor, humanity faces the risk of becoming "redundant," potentially pacified by digital "bread and circuses" (short-form content).
